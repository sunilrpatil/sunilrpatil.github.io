About

Dr. Sunil R. Patil works in computational condensed matter physics and molecular electronics, focusing on quantum transport in DNA and nanoscale systems.

His research integrates molecular dynamics, density functional theory, and non-equilibrium Green’s function methods to understand charge transport mechanisms.

Research Highlights

  • Environment-controlled DNA conductance
  • Decoherence in molecular systems
  • Multiscale MD + DFT + NEGF modeling
