About

We investigate charge transport in molecular and biological systems, with a focus on DNA nanoelectronics and quantum transport phenomena. Our research combines multiscale approaches including molecular dynamics, density functional theory, and non-equilibrium Green’s function methods.

Focus: Understanding how environment, structure, and quantum effects control electronic transport at the nanoscale.

Research Areas

  • Quantum Transport: Coherent and decoherent transport in nanoscale systems
  • DNA Nanoelectronics: Charge transport in biological molecules
  • Decoherence: Environmental effects and ionic interactions
  • Multiscale Modeling: MD + DFT + NEGF frameworks
